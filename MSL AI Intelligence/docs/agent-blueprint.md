# Agent Blueprint

## Mission

Create a synthetic Medical Science Liaison for thoracic oncology that transforms evidence into strategic trial ideation.

## Core Loop

1. Frame the decision.
2. Gather evidence from trials, RWE, guidelines, regulatory labels, HTA, and competitive sources.
3. Extract endpoint, subgroup, safety, access, and implementation signals.
4. Detect unmet needs and evidence gaps.
5. Match opportunities to product SWOT.
6. Generate and score trial concepts.
7. Translate the best concept into stakeholder-specific narratives.

## Agent Capabilities

- Scientific acumen: trial interpretation, endpoint hierarchy, subgroup logic, biomarker segmentation, safety interpretation.
- Business acumen: differentiation, access relevance, market and pathway implications.
- Cross-functional translation: KOL, HCP, patient, advocacy, payer, buyer, vendor, business partner, and internal medical affairs language.
- Strategic intelligence: evidence-based pitch creation, opportunity ranking, and claim substantiation.

## MVP Scope

- Disease: NSCLC.
- Primary use case: metastatic disease opportunity generation.
- Inputs: disease setting, product or class, SWOT, geography, available evidence sources.
- Outputs: evidence table, unmet-need cards, opportunity scorecard, trial concept canvas, stakeholder narratives, elevator pitch.

## Future Extensions

- Automated source ingestion from PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, conference abstracts, regulatory databases, and HTA sources.
- Structured trial extraction into a local evidence store.
- RWE import from internal data assets.
- Competitor monitoring and alerting.
- Presentation and briefing generation for medical affairs planning.
