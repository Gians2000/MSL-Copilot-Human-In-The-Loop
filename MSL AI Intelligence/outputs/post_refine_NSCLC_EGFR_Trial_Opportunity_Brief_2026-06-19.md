## Current evidence landscape

### Observed evidence

- - [PMID:41104938] FLAURA2 final analysis reported an OS benefit for osimertinib plus chemotherapy.
- - [FDA:MARIPOSA-2024] FDA approved lazertinib plus amivantamab-vmjw for first-line EGFR exon19del/L858R NSCLC.
- - [FDA:LAURA-2024] FDA approved osimertinib after platinum-based chemoradiotherapy for unresectable stage III EGFR exon19del/L858R NSCLC without progression.
- - [FDA:MARIPOSA2-2024] FDA approved amivantamab-vmjw plus carboplatin and pemetrexed after progression on or after an EGFR TKI.
- - [PMID:37689979] HERTHENA-Lung01 reported activity of patritumab deruxtecan after EGFR TKI and platinum-based chemotherapy.
- - [PMID:38958845] Real-world outcomes after osimertinib and platinum-based chemotherapy remain poor in reported US cohorts.
- - [PMID:41130408, NCT03944772] ORCHARD reported activity for osimertinib plus savolitinib in MET-amplified disease after first-line osimertinib.

### Interpretation

- evidenza non identificata

### Hypotheses

- - [PMID:38958845, FDA:MARIPOSA2-2024, PMID:41130408, NCT03944772] A post-osimertinib molecular assignment platform is a strategic hypothesis for EGFR-mutated NSCLC.
- - [PMID:41104938, FDA:MARIPOSA-2024] A first-line risk-adapted intensification trial is a strategic hypothesis for high-risk EGFR-mutated NSCLC.
- - [PMID:38042525] A CNS-first endpoint strategy is a strategic hypothesis for EGFR-mutated NSCLC trial design.

### Recommendations

- - [PMID:41235190] Economics-oriented evidence generation should capture resource use, PRO, and access-sensitive endpoints.

## SWOT matching

- Strengths: high unmet need after osimertinib and platinum, multiple source-linked therapeutic mechanisms, clear human-in-the-loop evidence ledger
- Weaknesses: strategic hypotheses require human validation, local label and reimbursement status are not verified in this PoC
- Opportunities: post-osimertinib biomarker-directed platform, first-line high-risk intensification algorithm, CNS-first endpoint design, embedded health economics data capture
- Threats: unsupported cross-trial superiority comparisons, off-label interpretation risk, rapidly shifting comparator landscape

## Trial opportunity hypothesis

- Hypothesis: post-osimertinib EGFR-mutated NSCLC should be developed as a molecularly assigned disease state, with tissue plus ctDNA-driven modules and embedded access endpoints.
- Hypothesis: first-line intensification should be tested through risk-adapted selection rather than assumed for every EGFR-mutated patient.
- Hypothesis: CNS protection should be elevated from exploratory subgroup analysis to a design objective in selected EGFR-mutated NSCLC trials.
